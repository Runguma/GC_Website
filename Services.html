SERVICES.

Land use planning

advisory/services

Undertaking socio-economic studies and surveys (Housing, industrial, commercial, transport, infrastructure, economic, socio-cultural, institutional/legal e.t.c) and preparation of urban development plans. This includes Long Term and Short-Term physical development plans, including preparation of housing site layout plans, sub-division schemes, land amalgamation, change of use, extension of use, extension/renewal of lease etc.



Land Survey

advisory/services

Land survey and administration has overly been the inception of different developments and activities relating to land. The consultancy had been diversly been involved and engaged in different survey works at different levels this within Machakos, Kitui, Kiambu and Makueni Counties.
